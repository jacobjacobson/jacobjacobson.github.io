Our Parsing Project is actively applying Morphology codes to the text of the OSHB. You can view the progress of the project at OSHB Parsing Status or see how each book is progressing at Book Status.

We are looking for Contributors skilled in Hebrew grammar, who will be able to parse portions of the text. An even greater need, at this time, is for people with more advanced familiarity with Hebrew, who would be able to serve as Editors, to verify parsing done by our Contributors. Anyone interested should go to the Parsing Project and register.

For more information, see the Hebrew Morphology Codes, that has a Beginner’s Guide and FAQ. Once you are registered, the parsing website has a Help section, to get you started. Questions can be posted to the OSHB Project Email List.
